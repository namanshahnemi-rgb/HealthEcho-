*   **Breakfast (420 kcal, 15 min prep): Berry Almond Power Oatmeal**
    *   1/2 cup rolled oats cooked with 1 cup unsweetened almond milk.

    *   Filling: 1 cup mashed chickpeas, 1/4 cup chopped cucumber, 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper, 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt (2% fat), 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp fresh dill (or 1 tsp dried), salt and pepper to taste.
    *   2 large lettuce leaves for crunch.
    *   *Cooking Tip: Mash chickpeas roughly with a fork ‚Äì don't worry about perfect smoothness! This salad can be prepped ahead.*

    *   1/4 cup walnuts.
