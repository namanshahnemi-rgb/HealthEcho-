### **üíß Hydration & Timing Recommendations üíß**

*   **Hydration is Key:** Aim for 8-10 glasses (2-2.5 liters) of water daily. Keep a reusable water bottle handy!
*   **Pre-Workout:** Hydrate well 1-2 hours before your workout. A small piece of fruit or a rice cake 30-60 minutes prior can provide quick energy.
*   **Post-Workout:** Aim to have your post-workout meal (often lunch or dinner) within 60-90 minutes to aid muscle recovery. If your workout is much earlier, a protein shake or your planned mid-morning snack could work well.
*   **Listen to Your Body:** Eat when you're hungry, stop when you're satisfied. This plan is a guide, not a rigid rulebook.

### **‚öñÔ∏è How to Adjust for Weight Changes ‚öñÔ∏è**

This plan is a fantastic starting point! Your body is unique, and progress isn't always linear.

*   **If Weight Loss Stalls:**
    *   **Reduce by 100-200 calories:** Consider slightly reducing portion sizes of healthy fats (e.g., 1/2 avocado instead of 1/4, 1 tbsp olive oil instead of 1.5 tbsp) or complex carbohydrates (e.g., 3/4 cup cooked rice instead of 1 cup).
    *   **Increase Activity:** Add an extra 15-30 minutes of walking or low-intensity cardio.
    *   **Ensure Accuracy:** Double-check your portion sizes ‚Äì it's easy for them to creep up!
*   **If You Feel Too Hungry/Low Energy:**
    *   **Add 100-200 calories:** Incorporate an extra serving of vegetables, a small handful of nuts, or an additional piece of fruit. Ensure you're getting enough protein.
    *   **Re-evaluate Sleep & Stress:** These can significantly impact hunger and energy levels.
*   **Consult a Professional:** If you're consistently struggling, don't hesitate to reach out to a dietitian for personalized guidance!

### **‚òÄÔ∏è Day 1: Fresh Start & Power Fuel ‚òÄÔ∏è**

*   **Breakfast (450 calories, 10 min prep)**
    *   **Meal:** Berry & Almond Butter Greek Yogurt Bowl
    *   **Portion:** 1 cup (220g) plain 0% fat Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen), 2 tbsp almond butter, 1 tbsp chia seeds.

*   **Afternoon Snack (250 calories)**
    *   **Meal:** 2 hard-boiled eggs, 1 medium orange.
    *   **Cooking Tip:** Hard-boil a batch of eggs on Sunday!
*   **Dinner (670 calories, 25 min prep)**
    *   **Meal:** Lean Turkey & Black Bean Chili with Brown Rice
    *   **Portion:** 150g lean ground turkey chili (made with canned tomatoes, black beans, corn, onions, peppers, chili powder), 1 cup cooked brown rice. Top with a dollop of 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt.
    *   **Cooking Tip:** Brown the turkey, then add chopped veggies and spices, then canned goods. Simmer for 15-20 min.
